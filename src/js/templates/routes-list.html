Instabus is no longer actively maintained

Thank you for your support and for advocating to CapMetro the need for realtime transit data. Thanks to your help, CapMetro noticed Instabus and invited us to work with them to publish realtime data. CapMetro now publishes reliable GTFS-realtime feeds, which means more than just the CapMetro app and Instabus can show where the bus is and when it will arrive.

Instead of Instabus, you should try out Transit, possibly the best realtime transit app that works in Austin.

If you're interested in maintaining Instabus, checkout the source code on GitHub or send me an email.
